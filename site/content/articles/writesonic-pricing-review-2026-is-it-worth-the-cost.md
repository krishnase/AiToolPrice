## Writesonic: Full Pricing Review

### Overview

Writesonic has established itself as one of the more versatile AI writing platforms on the market, offering tools for long-form content, ad copy, landing pages, and more. Its 2026 pricing structure keeps things relatively straightforward with three distinct tiers: a free entry point, an individual plan, and a team-focused option. The platform leans into GPT-4 access and unlimited word generation as key selling points at its paid tiers.

---

### Pricing Breakdown

Writesonic offers three plans in 2026:

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Savings |
|------|--------------|--------------|---------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | — |
| Individual | $20.00/month | $192.00/year | 20% |
| Teams | $30.00/month | $288.00/year | 20% |

**Free Plan** gives users access to **10,000 words per month** — a generous starting point for casual users or those testing the platform before committing.

**Individual Plan** at **$20/month** (or **$192/year**) removes the word cap entirely with unlimited word generation and adds access to **GPT-4**, Writesonic's most capable AI model. The 20% annual discount makes it a solid deal for consistent solo users.

**Teams Plan** at **$30/month** (or **$288/year**) builds on the Individual tier by adding **3 seats** and **Custom AI Voices**, making it geared toward small content teams that want brand consistency across their output.

## How to Choose the Right Writesonic Plan

**Start with the Free plan** if you're new to AI writing tools or only need occasional help with content. The 10,000-word monthly limit is a practical trial that doesn't require a credit card.

**Upgrade to Individual** when you find yourself hitting the word cap regularly or when content quality matters enough to justify GPT-4 access. At $20/month, it's one of the more affordable unlimited-word plans available.

**Choose Teams** if you're collaborating with two or more colleagues and need brand voice consistency through Custom AI Voices. At just $10 more per month than Individual, the per-seat value is strong for small groups.

**Go annual** whenever possible — both paid plans offer a clean 20% discount, which adds up to real savings over a year of consistent use.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the Writesonic Free plan require a credit card?**
A: No. The Free plan is accessible without payment details, making it a truly risk-free way to explore the platform within the 10,000 words/month limit.

**Q: Is GPT-4 included in all Writesonic paid plans?**
A: GPT-4 is available on the Individual plan ($20/month) and the Teams plan ($30/month). It is not included in the Free tier.

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing on Writesonic?**
