# Best AI Image Generator Pricing in 2024: Plans, Costs & Which Is Right for You

Choosing an AI image generator isn't just about the quality of the output — it's about finding a plan that fits your workflow, budget, and creative volume. Whether you're a hobbyist experimenting with AI art on weekends or a professional creative needing thousands of images monthly, pricing structures vary significantly across platforms.

In this guide, we evaluate Midjourney's pricing tiers across four key criteria: **value for money**, **output volume**, **feature access**, and **scalability**. Here's everything you need to make an informed decision.

---

## Midjourney Pricing Review

### Overview

Midjourney is widely regarded as one of the most powerful and artistically sophisticated AI image generators available. Operating primarily through Discord, it uses a GPU-hour model rather than a simple image credit system — meaning the time your jobs take to render affects how far your plan goes. All plans include access to Midjourney's core generation model, commercial usage rights, and the ability to generate images in varying aspect ratios and styles.

Importantly, **every plan comes with a 20% annual discount** when billed yearly, making it a strong choice for committed users.

### Plan Breakdown

**Basic — $10.00/month ($96.00/year)**
The Basic plan is Midjourney's entry point, offering approximately 200 images per month powered by 3.3 GPU hours. It's a lean but functional option for casual users who want to explore the platform without a significant financial commitment.

**Standard — $30.00/month ($288.00/year)**
The Standard plan triples the GPU allocation to 15 hours per month and introduces **Relax mode** — a game-changing feature that allows unlimited image generation at slower speeds. For most creatives and freelancers, Standard hits the sweet spot between cost and capability.

**Pro — $60.00/month ($576.00/year)**
Pro doubles the Fast GPU hours to 30 per month and adds **Stealth mode**, which keeps your generated images private and hidden from Midjourney's public gallery. This is critical for professionals working on confidential client projects or proprietary creative work.

**Mega — $120.00/month ($1,152.00/year)**
The Mega plan is built for power users and teams, offering 60 GPU hours per month alongside Stealth mode. At this level, output volume is rarely a constraint — making it ideal for agencies, studios, or high-frequency commercial users.

## How to Choose the Right Midjourney Plan

**Start with your volume needs.** If you're generating images casually — a few dozen per month — the Basic plan at $10.00/month is a sensible starting point. However, if image generation is central to your workflow, the Standard plan's Relax mode essentially removes the ceiling on output at $30.00/month.

**Consider privacy requirements.** If you're working on client deliverables, unreleased products, or proprietary branding, Stealth mode (available on Pro and Mega) is non-negotiable. That feature alone justifies the jump to $60.00/month for commercial users.

**Factor in annual savings.** Every plan saves 20% when billed annually. On the Mega plan, that's a saving of $288.00 per year — significant for studios budgeting creative tools at scale.

## FAQ

**Q: Does Midjourney offer a free plan?**
No. As of 2024, Midjourney no longer offers a free trial. The lowest entry point is the Basic plan at $10.00/month.

**Q: What is Relax mode and why does it matter?**
Relax mode allows Standard, Pro, and Mega subscribers to generate images without consuming GPU hours, at slower queue speeds. For high-volume users, this effectively makes image generation unlimited.

**Q: Can I switch plans mid-month?**
Yes, Midjourney allows users to upgrade or downgrade plans at any time, with billing adjustments applied on a prorated basis.
