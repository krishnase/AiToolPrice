# GitHub Copilot Pricing Plans Explained

**Note:** This article focuses on Midjourney pricing plans, as that is the tool data provided for this roundup.

---

AI image generation has exploded in popularity, and Midjourney remains one of the most powerful tools in the space. But choosing the right plan can feel overwhelming — especially when your needs sit somewhere between "casual experimenter" and "full-time creative professional." We evaluated Midjourney's four pricing tiers based on **image output limits, GPU hours, privacy features, and overall value for money** to help you find the plan that fits your workflow.

---

## Midjourney Pricing Plans: Full Breakdown

Midjourney operates on a subscription model built around **GPU hours** — the processing time used to generate your images. More GPU hours means more images, faster generation, and access to premium features. Every plan also comes with a **20% discount when billed annually**, making the yearly commitment worth serious consideration for regular users.

---

## Basic Plan — $10/month

**Summary:** The entry-level tier designed for casual users who want to explore Midjourney without a heavy financial commitment.

**Pricing:**
- $10.00/month (monthly billing)
- $96.00/year (save 20% annually)

**What you get:**
- 200 images per month
- 3.3 GPU hours

**Pros:**
- Lowest cost of entry
- Solid image cap for light use
- Annual plan brings the effective monthly cost to $8.00

**Cons:**
- No Relax mode — every generation draws from your GPU allowance
- Limited GPU hours burn quickly if you experiment heavily
- No privacy (Stealth) features

**Best for:** Hobbyists, beginners, or anyone testing Midjourney before committing to a higher tier.

---

## Standard Plan — $30/month

**Summary:** The most popular tier for regular creators, offering a significant jump in GPU hours plus the valuable Relax mode.

**Pricing:**
- $30.00/month (monthly billing)
- $288.00/year (save 20% annually)

**What you get:**
- 15 GPU hours/month (Fast mode)
- Unlimited Relax mode generations

**Pros:**
- Relax mode allows unlimited image generation (at slower speeds) — a huge value add
- Suitable for consistent daily use
- Annual plan reduces cost to $24.00/month effective

**Cons:**
- Still no Stealth mode — images remain publicly visible in the Midjourney gallery
- Fast GPU hours can deplete quickly during intensive projects

**Best for:** Content creators, social media managers, and designers who generate images regularly but don't require privacy.

---

## Pro Plan — $60/month

**Summary:** A professional-grade tier that unlocks privacy with Stealth mode and doubles the GPU allocation.

**Pricing:**
- $60.00/month (monthly billing)
- $576.00/year (save 20% annually)

**What you get:**
- 30 GPU hours/month (Fast mode)
- Relax mode
- **Stealth mode** (images hidden from public gallery)

**Pros:**
- Stealth mode is essential for client work or proprietary projects
- Double the fast GPU hours vs. Standard
- Annual billing brings effective monthly cost to $48.00

**Cons:**
- Significant price jump from Standard
- May be more than needed for users who don't require privacy

**Best for:** Freelance designers, agencies, and professionals handling confidential or commercial creative work.

---

## Mega Plan — $120/month

**Summary:** Midjourney's most powerful tier, built for power users and studios with extremely high image generation demands.

**Pricing:**
- $120.00/month (monthly billing)
- $1,152.00/year (save 20% annually)

**What you get:**
- 60 GPU hours/month (Fast mode)
- Relax mode
- Stealth mode

**Pros:**
- Maximum GPU hours available — ideal for high-volume production
- All premium features included
- Annual plan reduces cost to $96.00/month effective

**Cons:**
- Significant monthly investment
- Overkill for most individual users

**Best for:** Studios, creative teams, and power users running large-scale or commercial image production pipelines.

## How to Choose the Right Midjourney Plan

**Choose Basic if** you're new to AI image generation and want to experiment without overspending. The 200-image cap is enough to learn the platform.

**Choose Standard if** you create images regularly and want the freedom of unlimited Relax mode generations. It's the best balance of cost and output for most users.

**Choose Pro if** your work involves client deliverables or commercially sensitive projects where image privacy is non-negotiable.

**Choose Mega if** you're running a creative studio or production environment where high-volume, fast image generation is a daily operational requirement.

One universal tip: **always consider the annual plan.** Every tier saves exactly 20% with yearly billing, which adds up significantly at the Pro and Mega levels.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I switch plans mid-month on Midjourney?**
Yes. Midjourney allows you to upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades typically apply at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I run out of Fast GPU hours?**
On Standard, Pro, and Mega plans, you can continue generating images using Relax mode at no extra cost — just at slower speeds. On the Basic plan, you would need to wait for your hours to reset or purchase additional GPU time.

**Q: Is the annual plan worth it?**
For any user generating images consistently, yes. The 20% discount is applied uniformly across all tiers — saving you $24/year on Basic, $72/year on Standard, $144/year on Pro, and $288/year on Mega.
