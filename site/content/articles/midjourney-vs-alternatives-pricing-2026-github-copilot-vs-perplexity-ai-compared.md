## GitHub Copilot: Pricing and Features Breakdown

GitHub Copilot is purpose-built for software developers, and its tiered pricing reflects that focused audience clearly.

The **Free tier** delivers a genuinely useful 2,000 code completions per month — enough for casual contributors or developers exploring the tool before committing. For serious coders, the **Individual plan at $10.00/month** (or $100.00/year, saving 17%) unlocks unlimited completions alongside chat functionality, making it one of the most competitive entry points in AI-assisted development.

Teams and organizations have two options. The **Business plan at $19.00/month** adds policy management and audit logs — critical features for companies that need compliance oversight. Stepping up to the **Enterprise plan at $39.00/month** brings custom model support and advanced security capabilities, designed for large engineering organizations with sophisticated infrastructure needs.

It's worth noting that the Business and Enterprise plans carry no annual discount, so organizations should factor that into budget planning when comparing monthly versus annual costs.

**Bottom line:** GitHub Copilot offers exceptional value for individual developers, particularly at the $10/month tier. The free plan is one of the more generous on the market for a coding-specific tool.

---

## Perplexity AI: Pricing and Features Breakdown

Perplexity AI takes a fundamentally different approach — it's an AI-powered answer engine designed for research, exploration, and staying informed, rather than writing code.

The **Free tier** is remarkably capable, offering unlimited searches powered by GPT-4o mini. For casual users or those with light research needs, this alone may be sufficient. However, users who want access to more powerful models will need to upgrade.

The **Pro plan at $20.00/month** (or $200.00/year, saving 17%) unlocks 300 Pro searches per day using premium models including Claude and GPT-4o. For researchers, students, journalists, or business analysts who rely heavily on accurate, sourced information, this access to frontier models represents a meaningful productivity upgrade.

Unlike GitHub Copilot, Perplexity currently offers only two tiers, making the decision straightforward — though it also means there's no tailored option for enterprise teams with administrative needs.

**Bottom line:** Perplexity AI's free tier punches well above its weight. The Pro plan is slightly pricier than Copilot's individual tier but justifies its cost for high-volume research users.
