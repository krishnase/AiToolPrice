## How to Reach Us

**Email:** contact@aitoolprice.com

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

---

## What to Contact Us About

### Pricing Corrections
Found an outdated or incorrect price on the Site? Please include:
- The tool name and plan
- The correct price (with a link to the official pricing page if possible)
- The article URL where you spotted the error

### Tool Suggestions
Want us to cover an AI tool we haven't reviewed yet? Tell us the tool name and why you think it would be useful for our readers.

### Press & Media
For media inquiries, partnership opportunities, or advertising questions, email us at **contact@aitoolprice.com** with "Press" in the subject line.

### Affiliate & Sponsorship
We accept limited, clearly-disclosed sponsorship arrangements that do not influence our editorial rankings. Email us with "Sponsorship" in the subject line.
