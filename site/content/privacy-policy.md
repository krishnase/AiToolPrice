## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

We do not require account registration. If you contact us via email or a contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily submit (such as your name and email address) solely to respond to your inquiry.

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, our servers and third-party services may automatically collect:

- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring URLs
- Device type and operating system
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

## 2. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors interact with the Site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

**We use cookies for:**

- **Analytics** — via Google Analytics (GA4), which collects anonymized usage data to help us understand traffic and improve the Site.
- **Advertising** — via Google AdSense, which may serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your browsing activity and interests.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the Site.

### Google AdSense

We participate in the Google AdSense program. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads on our Site based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or by visiting [aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).
