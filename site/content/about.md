## We Track AI Tool Prices So You Don't Have To

AI tools raise and lower their prices constantly — and the differences between plans are rarely obvious. **AI Tool Price** exists to cut through the noise with clear, accurate, up-to-date pricing data and honest comparisons.

We launched because we were frustrated with the same problem every AI tool buyer faces: pricing pages that hide the real cost, annual-vs-monthly traps, and "contact sales" plans that tell you nothing. We built the tracker we wished existed.

---

## What We Do

**AI Tool Price** monitors pricing for 50+ AI tools — including ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper AI, Grammarly, Copy.ai, Writesonic, Midjourney, and more — and publishes:

- **Side-by-side comparison articles** for the most common "Tool A vs Tool B" decisions
- **Roundup guides** for specific use cases (writing, coding, image generation, students, teams)
- **Price change alerts** when a tool raises or lowers its pricing
- **Plain-English breakdowns** of what each plan actually includes

All pricing data is verified manually against each tool's official pricing page and updated regularly.

---

## Our Editorial Standards

We believe AI tool coverage should be honest, not sponsored. Here is how we maintain that:

- **No pay-to-rank**: Tools cannot pay to appear higher in our comparisons. Rankings are based on value and features.
- **Affiliate disclosure**: Some links may be affiliate links (see our [Terms](/terms/)). This does not affect our recommendations.
- **Data accuracy**: All prices are sourced directly from official pricing pages. We note when prices are estimates or require contacting sales.
- **Regular updates**: Our pipeline checks pricing data daily. Articles are refreshed whenever a price changes.
