❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI tool is cheapest in 2026?

GitHub Copilot starts at $10/month, making it the cheapest paid AI tool for developers. For general use, Writesonic's Individual plan at $20/month offers unlimited words — the best value for content creators. Most tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Grammarly, Perplexity) also offer solid free tiers.

Does ChatGPT have a free version?

Yes. ChatGPT's free tier gives you access to GPT-4o mini with limited GPT-4o access. It's one of the most capable free AI tools available. The Plus plan at $20/month unlocks full GPT-4o, DALL-E 3 image generation, and advanced data analysis.

Is it worth paying for an AI tool when free versions exist?

For frequent users, yes. Paid plans typically remove daily usage limits, unlock more powerful models, and add features like plagiarism checking or image generation. If you use AI daily for work, the time saved usually exceeds the monthly cost.

How often do AI tool prices change?

More often than most people realize. Several major tools changed their pricing in 2025 alone. We monitor pricing daily and publish price change articles whenever a tool raises or lowers its plans. Subscribe to get notified.

Which AI tool is best for students on a tight budget?

Start with the free tiers: ChatGPT for general tasks, Perplexity AI for research with citations, and Claude for long-form writing. All three have generous free plans that cover most academic use cases. See our full guide to cheapest AI tools for students.
